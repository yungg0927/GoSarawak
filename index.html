  • To highlight famous tourist attractions in Sarawak.
  • To promote the development of Sarawak's tourism industry

About Sarawak

Sarawak is Malaysia's largest state, located on the island of Borneo. Sarawak consists of 5 cities, which are Kuching, Miri, Sibu, and Bintulu. Additionally, the capital of Sarawak is Kuching. Its rich natural resources, strategic location, rapidly developing infrastructure, and forward-looking national policies make it attractive to business investors.
